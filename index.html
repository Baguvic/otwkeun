Mt. Tampomas, Cibereum

Mount Tampomas is a volcano located in West Java, Indonesia, with an elevation of approximately 1,684 meters above sea level. The name "Tampomas" comes from the Sundanese language, meaning "looks higher" or "appears taller." This mountain is a stratovolcano with an interesting geological history.

In the hidden hinterlands of Sumedang, lies Puncak Damar and its surroundings, including Jatigede Dam.
